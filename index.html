Michael Cowley

Astrophysicist.

Lecturer at the Queensland University of Technology and Adjunct Research Fellow at the University of Southern Queensland. Michael researches extragalactic astronomy and cosmology, with a focus on galaxy evolution and supermassive black holes. Michael is an active science communicator having contributed to documentaries, local and national media interviews, and as a TEDx speaker. Michael has also been published in a number of popular publications, such as The Conversation, Cosmos Magazine, and the Australasian Science Magazine.
